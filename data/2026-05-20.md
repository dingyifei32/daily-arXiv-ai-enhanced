### [1] [Mapping 3-D Explosive Nucleosynthesis with Type II Supernova Infrared Emission Lines](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18951)
*W. V. Jacobson-Galán,L. Dessart,D. Vartanyan*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文通过光学和红外光谱（含JWST数据）分析SN 2024ggi在~265-400天阶段的星云光谱，发现不同铁族元素发射线呈现双峰或高斯轮廓，表明内层抛射物存在化学不均匀性和非球形电离。双峰轮廓建模支持Ni/Co/Fe富集区极向增强达~7倍。LTE估算稳定Ni质量约1.3×10⁻³ M☉，但电子密度接近临界值暗示偏离LTE。非LTE辐射传输模型倾向于前身星质量~12-15.2 M☉。对比3D中微子驱动爆炸模拟发现，只有高能3D爆炸模型的高质量前身星才能再现观测的Ni混合程度，与辐射传输模型推断的前身星质量矛盾。研究揭示了星云红外线作为核塌缩超新星爆炸合成物质3D分布直接探针的价值。

Abstract: We present analysis and modeling of optical and infrared (IR) spectroscopy of the Type II supernova (SN II) 2024ggi obtained with ground-based instruments and the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) at phases of ~265 - 400 days. ### [2] [Automating the detection of polarization angle rotations in blazars. Re-analysis of RoboPol data reveals 27 new rotations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18955)
*Anastasia Glykopoulou,Ioannis Liodakis,Dmitry Blinov*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种自动化检测耀变体EVPA旋转的流程，集成了180°去模糊、贝叶斯块分割和统计验证。应用于RoboPol数据后，识别出25个源的48次旋转，发现贝叶斯块方法检测到的旋转振幅比人工方法大约10%，持续时间长约2倍，角速度慢约2/3。新报告27个此前未记录的旋转，并发现较长旋转与增强的γ射线活动相关。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有EVPA旋转检测依赖人工分割，存在主观性强、可重复性差的问题，且不同方法之间的系统偏差尚未被量化。因此需要一种自动化、客观、可复现的检测流程。

Method: 提出自动化管线：先校正180°模糊性，再采用贝叶斯块（Bayesian Blocks）进行自适应分割，最后进行统计验证。应用于RoboPol监测数据进行检测，并与已有目录比较，同时分析Fermi-LAT γ射线光变曲线的相关性。

Abstract: We present an automated pipeline for the detection of EVPA rotations in blazars, integrating correction of the 180$^\circ$ ambiguity, Bayesian Blocks segmentation, and statistical validation. Applied to RoboPol monitoring data, the method identified 48 rotations across 25 sources, including multiple events in RBPLJ2232+1143, RBPLJ1751+0939, RBPLJ1800+7828, and RBPLJ2253+1608. A correlation analysis with contemporaneous Fermi--LAT $γ$-ray light curves shows that longer rotations tend to coincide with enhanced $γ$-ray activity, while rotation amplitude alone is not predictive of $γ$-ray brightness. Our pipeline minimizes subjective biases, expands the list of known EVPA rotations, and provides a reproducible framework for future multiwavelength studies of blazar jet dynamics and particle acceleration.
