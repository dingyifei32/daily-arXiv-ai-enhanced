Motivation: 脉冲星风云（PWNe）是甚高能伽马射线波段的重要辐射源，但其中年老PWNe的长期演化机制尚不明确——这类系统可能是银河系TeV辐射的主要贡献者。现有理论模型对回响阶段的动力学行为存在不确定性，且缺乏对多维效应的系统评估，因此需要验证当前一维模拟的可靠性，为即将到来的ASTRI和CTA等天文台提供可靠的理论预测。

Predicting their long-term evolution is crucial for forthcoming high-energy observatories like ASTRI and CTA. To understand the diversity of these systems, we derive the Pulsar and Supernova Remnant (SNR) parameters governing PWN evolution. We find that instability growth depends on initial perturbations but does not significantly alter global dynamics. For the first time, we investigated in detail the multidimensional evolution of middle-aged PWNe in the reverberation phase.
