### [1] [Particle Acceleration, Coronal Neutrino Production, and the Diffuse Extragalactic Neutrino Background from Supermassive Black Holes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13968)
*Rostom Mbarek*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文提出了一个广义中微子光度函数模型，用于描述 Seyfert 类星系中超大质量黑洞 X 射线冕内加速质子产生的中微子。研究表明，这些系统可以解释 IceCube 观测到的亚 PeV 弥漫河外中微子通量，而中微子光度主要取决于冕的 X 射线光度和磁化强度，与黑洞质量关系较弱。此外，磁场拓扑结构可能允许宇宙线驱动的外流，产生 PeV 级中微子并影响星系内部环境动力学。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 评估 Seyfert 星系黑洞 X 射线冕中质子加速产生中微子的贡献，主要不确定性在于粒子加速物理机制，本文旨在通过等离子体动力学模拟提供更自洽的理论框架。

Method: 基于等离子体动力学模拟的理论加速框架，建立冕条件、非热质子种群与中微子产生之间的自洽联系，推导广义中微子光度函数。

Result: 中微子光度主要取决于冕的 X 射线光度和磁化强度，与黑洞质量关系较弱；宇宙学积分辐射可解释 IceCube 观测的亚 PeV 弥漫河外中微子通量。

Conclusion: 黑洞附近的磁场拓扑允许宇宙线驱动的外流，可能在 PeV 能级产生额外中微子，并影响星系最内部环境的动力学过程。

Abstract: We present a generalized neutrino luminosity function for protons accelerated in the X-ray coronae of supermassive black holes in Seyfert-like galaxies. A major uncertainty in assessing the diffuse neutrino contribution of these systems is the underlying particle acceleration physics. We address this using a theoretical acceleration framework informed by plasma kinetic simulations, enabling a more self-consistent connection between coronal conditions, nonthermal proton populations, and neutrino production. In this picture, the neutrino luminosity depends primarily on the coronal X-ray luminosity and magnetization, and only weakly on black hole mass. We find that the cosmologically integrated emission from these systems can account for the sub-PeV diffuse extragalactic neutrino flux observed by IceCube. We further argue that, although diffusive confinement is relatively well understood, the magnetic field topology near black holes naturally allows for cosmic ray-driven outflows near the X-ray corona. Such outflows may accompany additional efficient neutrino production at the PeV-level and influence the dynamics of the innermost galactic environment.

### [2] [Planets in Pulsar Winds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14077)
*T. Kaister,S. Andrés Joya Méndez,P. Marmat,M. Čemeljić,M. Velli,J. Varela,M. Falanga*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文提出通过脉冲星-行星磁层相互作用产生的射电辐射来探测脉冲星行星，并通过相对论数值模拟证明已知行星PSR J0636+5129 b可被探测到。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 脉冲星行星目前主要通过脉冲星计时法发现，本文探索利用射电辐射作为新的探测手段。

Method: 采用特殊相对论数值模拟，将行星建模为完美导体表面，置于脉冲星风中（v=0.985c, γ=5.795），行星在脉冲星磁场中在夜侧产生延展磁结构并发出射电辐射。

Result: 已知脉冲星PSR J0636+5129 b可通过其射电辐射被探测到。

Conclusion: 脉冲星-行星磁层相互作用产生的射电辐射是一种可行的脉冲星行星探测新方法，并具有观测前景。

Abstract: Planets around pulsars were the first discovered exoplanets, found thanks to the extremely precise pulsar timing. Here we suggest that they could also be found through the radio emission generated by the pulsar-planet magnetospheric interaction. We present the results of special relativistic numerical simulations of planets in a pulsar wind of velocity $v=0.985~c$, corresponding to a Lorentz factor $γ=5.795$. Planets, modeled as a perfectly conducting solid surface in an external magnetic field originating from the pulsar, produce radio emission in the extended magnetic structure on the planet's nightside. We find that the planet around a known pulsar, PSR J0636+5129 b, could be detected via its radio emission. We outline the observational prospects for such objects.

</details>
