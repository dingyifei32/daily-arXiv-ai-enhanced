Simulations of accretion onto regular loop-quantum black holes show that quantum-gravity corrections can modify photon-ring size, polarization structure, and jet power, leading to observational constraints from Event Horizon Telescope data. Together, these results connect relativistic plasma dynamics with current and future observations of black-hole systems.
