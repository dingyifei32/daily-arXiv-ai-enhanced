Motivation: 对SN 2025bvm进行多波段光学测光观测，以确定其光变曲线特征、光度、下降速率等物理性质，并判断其在Ia型超新星分类中的位置。

Method: 使用COLIBRI望远镜在OAN-SPM进行了121天的多波段（B、g、r、i）光学测光观测，测量光变曲线，计算Δm15(B)下降速率，校正宿主星系消光，并基于70Mpc的距离计算绝对星等。

Result: 测得Δm15(B)=0.867±0.051 mag（缓慢下降型），峰值绝对星等MB=-19.13±0.40 mag，光度与下降速率完全一致，属于正常Ia型超新星。光变曲线显示缓慢的晚期下降和显著的i波段次极大特征。

Conclusion: SN 2025bvm是一个正常的Ia型超新星，其缓慢的晚期光变下降和显著的i波段次极大特征表明该超新星爆炸产生了特别大质量的抛射物。

Abstract: We present 121 days of multi-band (\Bband, \gband, \rband, \iband) optical photometry of the Type Ia supernova SN 2025bvm, obtained with the COLIBRI telescope at OAN-SPM. The light curves show a photometric decline of $Δm_{15}(B) = 0.867 \pm 0.051$~mag, characteristic of a slow-declining Type Ia supernova. After correcting for host galaxy extinction ($E(B-V)_{host} = 0.308 \pm 0.030$~mag) and adopting a distance of 70~Mpc, we derive a peak absolute magnitude of $M_B = -19.13 \pm 0.40$~mag. This luminosity is fully consistent with its slow decline rate, placing SN 2025bvm within the population of normal Type Ia supernovae. We conclude that SN 2025bvm is a normal Type Ia supernova, whose photometric properties, such as a slow late-time decline and a prominent \iband-band secondary maximum, suggest an explosion that resulted in a particularly massive ejecta.
