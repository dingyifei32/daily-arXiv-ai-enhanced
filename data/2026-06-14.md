Method: 通过数值模拟，计算太阳B-8中微子引起的CEvNS事件的核反冲通量/能量的三维角分布。

Result: CEvNS事件呈现特征性的环形角分布，具有明显的年度变化，且与WIMP的固定模式不同，也没有实验可区分的靶核依赖性。

Conclusion: 太阳中微子CEvNS信号的环形角分布年度变化特性可用于区分中微子背景与暗物质信号。

Abstract: Following our earlier work on studying 3-dimensional nuclear recoil response to Galactic Weakly Interacting Massive Particles (WIMPs) in directional direct Dark Matter detectors, in this paper, we simulate 3-D coherent elastic neutrino-nucleus scattering (CEvNS) events induced by Solar B-8 neutrinos. Our numerical results show that, in contrast to the approximately fixed patterns of the WIMP-induced signals, the characteristic ring-like angular distributions of the nuclear recoil flux/energy of CEvNS events show clearly annual variations along the trajectories of the moving direction of incident Solar neutrinos in different celestial coordinate systems without experimentally distinguishable target dependence.
